02 · About

Meet John.

I'm John. I live and work right here in the DFW area and I've been doing hands-on trade work for years. What started as plumbing turned into "hey John, can you also look at my fence / my yard / my garage floor?" — and here we are. I run a small crew and we cover most of what a homeowner needs without you having to call five different guys.

I keep it simple. I show up when I say I will, I tell you what the job actually costs before I start, and I don't leave a mess behind. A lot of my work comes from neighbors telling other neighbors, and I'd rather keep it that way than cut corners to chase the next job. If I can't do something right, I'll tell you and point you to someone who can.

Service area

I work across Frisco, Little Elm, Celina, and most of the surrounding DFW area — Prosper, McKinney, The Colony, Plano on a case-by-case basis. If you're a little outside that ring, call anyway. If I can't make it work I'll tell you up front and probably know somebody nearby who can.
