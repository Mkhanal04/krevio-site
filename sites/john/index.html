Handyman

Landscaping

Meet John

Questions I hear a lot

Straight answers.

How much do you charge?

It depends on the job, but I don't play games with pricing. After you tell me what's going on I can usually give you a ballpark on the phone or through the chat on this site, and then a firm number once I see it in person. No surprise line items at the end.

Who am I actually hiring?

Me. I live here, I run a small crew out of Frisco, and you'll have my number the whole way through the job. I'm local, accountable, and easy to reach — if something's off after I leave, I come back.

Can you come out today?

A lot of the time, yes — especially for leaks, no-power situations, or anything that's actively getting worse. Call or text and I'll tell you straight whether I can make it today or first thing tomorrow.

Do you handle emergencies after hours?

For active leaks, no-hot-water, or anything that's about to damage your house, call me and we'll figure it out. I don't promise 24/7 but I pick up most of the time.

How does the estimate work?

Tell me what's going on. I'll either quote it over the phone if it's straightforward, or swing by and take a look. On-site estimates are free for most jobs in my service area.

How far do you travel?

Frisco, Little Elm, Celina, and the surrounding DFW suburbs are my main area. Farther out is possible — just ask.

How do I pay?

Cash, check, Venmo, Zelle, and credit/debit cards. I'll send a clean invoice either way so you've got a record.

Do you warranty your work?

Yes. If something I fixed goes wrong because of how I fixed it, I come back and make it right. Warranty length depends on the job — I'll tell you before I start.

Do you handle big remodels or just small stuff?

Mostly small-to-mid jobs — repairs, installs, one-room projects, outdoor work. For full gut remodels I'll point you to someone who does that full-time. No ego about it.

Do you work weekends?

Saturdays yes, most Sundays no — that's family time. If it's an emergency, call anyway.
